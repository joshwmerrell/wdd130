WWR

White Water Rafting Site

My first class project. Because it was my first, it was already pre-designed in this course and I followed along to create it.

White Water Rafting was our theme, and the most difficult part of setting up the site was the logo and header.

Another big challenge for me was getting all of the images here to become square. I ended up putting them in divs and using aspect ratio.

Lastly, I was able to create an overlay effect between the last image and its blurb. By using grid and z-index, I could force elements to overlap eachother.
